Our Standards & Methodology

ATP employs a proprietary "Deep Listening" protocol that goes beyond traditional phone banking. We combine verified voter file data with real-time digital sentiment analysis to build a 360° view of the electorate.

01.

Sample Integrity

We strictly filter for likely voters using a historic 3-cycle model, ensuring our data reflects the actual electorate, not just the general population.

02.

Multi-Modal Intake

Data is ingested from SMS, web panels, and secure app integration, removing the bias of landline-only polling methods.

03.

Algorithmic Weighting

Our AI-driven weighting engine adjusts for demographic shifts in real-time, correcting for under-sampled cohorts dynamically.

Email Technical Specifications

Guidelines for building and sending emails that meet our brand and accessibility standards.

Email Structure & Content

HTML Structure

  • Max width: 600px centered.
  • Table-based layout for Outlook compatibility.
  • Inline CSS only (no external stylesheets).
  • Standard system fonts fallback stack.

Image Handling

  • All images must have alt text.
  • Logos: 2x resolution (Retina ready).
  • Hero images: Max file size 150KB.
  • Block images: display: block; border: 0;

Accessibility & Dark Mode

  • Contrast ratio 4.5:1 minimum.
  • Dark mode: Transparent PNGs required.
  • Font size: Min 16px body, 24px headers.
  • Tap targets: Min 44x44px.

Tone of Voice & Messaging

Our voice is authoritative, data-backed, and patriotic, but never alarmist.

The Navigator

We guide voters through noise with clarity and precision.

✓ Do: "Data indicates a shift in sentiment..."
✖ Don't: "You won't believe what happened..."
The Neighbor

We are rooted in the community, not just observing it.

✓ Do: "Families in North Hills are concerned..."
✖ Don't: "The voters in District 12..."
The Architect

We focus on solutions and structural improvements.

✓ Do: "This policy reduces congestion by 15%..."
✖ Don't: "This is a disaster waiting to happen."
The Scientist

We validate every claim with rigorous sourcing.

✓ Do: "According to our Q1 tracking poll (N=500)..."
✖ Don't: "Everyone knows that support is rising."
