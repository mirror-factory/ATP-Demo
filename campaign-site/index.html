3 Main Takeaways from District 12

We surveyed 12,847 neighbors across Westside, North Hills, and Downtown. These are the top priorities that demand immediate action. No spin. Just data.

Affordable Housing
87%

of residents support the new Affordable Housing Initiative when shown detailed cost-benefit analysis.

Action Plan

Re-zone transit corridors for mixed-income development. Mandate 20% affordable units in new construction projects over 50 units.

Public Schools
76%

agree that Public School Investment should be the #1 budget priority, ahead of stadiums or commercial development.

Action Plan

Allocate $12M from the general fund to reduce class sizes in North Hills schools from 32:1 to 24:1 student-teacher ratio.

Transit Expansion
68%

support extending the metro line to Westside, even with a small property tax increase ($120/year avg).

Action Plan

Fast-track Phase 2 Metro Extension. Prioritize connectivity over aesthetic design to reduce costs by 18%.
